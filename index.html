Privacy Policy for QR Studio

Last updated: September 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

QR Studio is designed to work offline first. We do not collect, store, or share personal data on our servers. However, the App may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Camera access: Used only to scan QR codes. We do not record, upload, or share camera data.
  • Storage access: Used if you choose to save QR codes or export them to your gallery.
  • App usage data: If crash reports or analytics are enabled through Google Play Services, Google may collect anonymized technical information such as device type, OS version, and performance logs.

2. How We Use Information

  • To scan QR codes using your device’s camera.
  • To save and manage QR codes locally on your device.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs (via anonymized crash reports and analytics if enabled).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. The App does not transmit scanned data to external servers unless you explicitly share a QR code using Android’s sharing features.

Third-party services used in the App may collect information in accordance with their own policies:

  • Google Play Services (for app distribution and crash reporting).
  • AdMob (if ads are displayed, they may collect advertising identifiers).

4. Security

All QR codes and saved history remain stored locally on your device unless you export or share them. We rely on the security features of your device’s operating system (e.g., Android sandboxing).

5. Children’s Privacy

QR Studio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on our GitHub Pages site: https://noviceplus.github.io/qr-studio-privacy-policy/
